Service Details: 12-Week Personal Training Program

Program Overview

Our signature 12-week personal training program is designed to transform your fitness level through consistent, professional coaching. This comprehensive program includes everything you need to succeed.

What's Included

  • 36 one-on-one training sessions (3 per week)
  • Initial comprehensive fitness assessment
  • Custom workout plan tailored to your goals
  • Weekly progress tracking and plan adjustments
  • Form and technique correction
  • Motivation and accountability support
  • Email access to your trainer between sessions
  • Digital progress photos and measurements

How It Works

  1. Week 1: Complete fitness assessment, goal setting, and program design
  2. Weeks 2-6: Foundation building with focus on proper form and baseline fitness
  3. Weeks 7-9: Progressive intensity increase and adaptation
  4. Weeks 10-12: Peak performance phase with strength and endurance testing
